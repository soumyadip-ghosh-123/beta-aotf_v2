# Provider Features

AOTF is designed to provide teachers and freelancers with a professional, transparent, and respectful gig-finding experience.

## 1. The Public Profile (`/u/[username]`)

Every provider gets a permanent, public-facing profile URL: `aotf.in/u/johndoe`.

This serves as a digital resume they can share with clients. It includes:
- **Bio & Identity**: Avatar, display name, and a short bio.
- **Qualifications**: Degrees, board preferences, teaching and job experience.
- **Subjects**: A list of subjects they are proficient in.
- **Links**: LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, or a personal website.

This profile is exactly what admins review when a provider applies for a post, making profile completeness critical.

## 2. The Job & Tuition Boards

Providers have access to live, filterable boards for Tuitions (`/posts`) and Freelance Jobs (`/jobs`).

- **Tuitions**: Filterable by Class Type (Online/Offline) and Text Search. Shows the budget, frequency, location, and student requirements.
- **Jobs**: Filterable by Work Type (Job/Project), Location Type (Remote/Onsite), and Gender preference.

The boards explicitly hide consumer contact information (phone number, exact name) to protect consumer privacy and prevent providers from bypassing the platform.

## 3. Real-Time Application Tracking

Providers are no longer left in the dark after applying. Their personal Dashboard shows the exact status of every application:

- **Applied**: The baseline status.
- **DC**: The admin is actively scheduling a demo call.
- **Approved**: They got the job.
- **Declined**: They were rejected, usually accompanied by a specific reason provided by the admin.
- **Auto-Declined**: The job was filled by someone else.

## 4. Feedback & Dispute Resolution

If a provider encounters an issue (e.g., a guardian isn't paying, an admin isn't responding, or they need to report bad behavior), they can submit a Feedback ticket directly from their dashboard.

This creates a tracked issue that the admin team must review and resolve, ensuring providers have a voice and a support channel.
